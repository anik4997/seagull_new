Refund Policy

Seagull accepts online returns of non-sale, unworn/unwashed merchandise with original tags attached within 30 calendar days from the date of delivery. All products must be returned in its original packaging and with all related accessories included. Items in gift sets may not be separated and must be returned complete in its original packaging. All refunds will be applied to the original payment method. If you return a package that does not meet this criterion, it will be denied. All denied returns will not receive a refund and will be forfeited. Original shipping fees are non-refundable.

All eBooks are Final Sale.

No returns or exchanges. Previous purchases are not eligible for discount. Sale items are ineligible for price adjustments.

All Subscriptions (eBooks and Electronic Journals) are Final Sale.

No returns or exchanges. Previous purchases are not eligible for discount. Sale items are ineligible for price adjustments.

Store Returns

You may choose to return any item that you have purchased online at one of our store locations within 30 calendar days from the date of delivery. Simply bring a copy of your original invoice that was included in your order package. We cannot accept returns without your original invoice. Your return will be exchanged for a Seagull gift card good at any of our Seagull store locations.

International Store Returns

Online purchases cannot be returned to Seagull international retail store locations.

Return Notifications

You will be notified via email once your return has been processed. Please note that this email will be sent to the email address provided on your original order. Your return credit will be placed on the original form of payment and will appear on your next statement (generally 10-14 days, depending on your bank).

Order Cancellation Policy

We want you to get your order as soon as possible, so we begin processing it immediately after you place it. Once your order has been submitted, we are unable to cancel or make changes to the order. Please review your order very carefully prior to clicking the 'Place Order' button on checkout page.

For more information, please contact our customer service department..
